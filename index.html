About GetMyGpa

What is GetMyGpa?

GetMyGpa is a simple and user-friendly single page application dedicated to aiding High School students in calculating and becoming more aware of their GPA. As of December of 2014, GetMyGpa is used by approximately 150 students on a regular basis.

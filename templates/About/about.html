About GetMyGpa

What is GetMyGpa?

GetMyGpa is a simple and user-friendly single page application dedicated to aiding students in calculating and becoming more aware of their weighted GPA. As of March of 2015, GetMyGpa is used by approximately 200 students on a regular basis ranging from several High Schools.


How do I use it?

  • Place your semester grade average for your 1st period class in the Grade box.

  • Place your corresponding grade weight in the Weight box. The general weight for corresponding classes are defined below.

    Weight Class Distinction
    6.0 Pre-AP/GT
    6.0 AP
    6.0 IB
    6.0 Dual Credit
    5.5 Advanced
    5.0 Regular

  • That class's gpa will be displayed directly underneath, updating as you type.

  • Your overall gpa will be displayed in the circle graph, in a circle.

  • When you would like to add a class, simply press the Add Class button or press Enter

  • If you need to delete a class, press the delete button Delete
